School Leaving Certificate (SLC) Tracking

The School Leaving Certificate is issued to students who are leaving the school. Parents/Guardians can track the status of their SLC application through the school office. Below is the process for applying for SLC:

  • Collect the SLC Application Form from the school office or download from the Downloads section.
  • Submit the duly filled form along with fee receipt and any pending dues clearance certificate.
  • SLC is typically issued within 7 working days of application submission.
  • For tracking status, contact the school office at gsssbhangrola2013@gmail.com.
  • Provisional SLC can be issued on request for admission purposes.

Note: All library books and school property must be returned before SLC is issued. Clearance from all departments is mandatory.
