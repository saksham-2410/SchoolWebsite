About the School

A Legacy of
Excellence in Education

PM SHRI Govt. Sr. Sec. School, Bhangrola, established under the prestigious PM SHRI (Prime Minister Schools for Rising India) scheme, stands as a beacon of quality education in the heart of Gurugram district. Situated in the serene environs of Bhangrola village, Block Farrukhnagar, the school proudly serves over 1,084 students from the surrounding communities.

Affiliated with the Haryana Board of School Education, the institution follows a holistic educational framework that nurtures academic excellence alongside personal growth. Our campus spans 33 Kanal 16 Marla, housing 27 well-ventilated classrooms & laboratories — including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, IT, and PCA (Patient Care) labs — and a richly stocked library, all designed to create an enriching learning environment.

Our vision is to empower every learner with knowledge, values, and skills that enable them to contribute meaningfully to society. We believe in developing not just academically sound individuals but compassionate, responsible citizens equipped for the challenges of the 21st century.

The school takes pride in its team of 34 dedicated educators whose passion for teaching has driven students to achieve 100% scores in board examinations. With 20+ zonal, district and block level recognitions, PM SHRI Govt. Sr. Sec. School, Bhangrola continues to grow in its mission to provide transformative education to every student who walks through its gates.
