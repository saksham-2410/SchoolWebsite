A Word From the District
Captain Indu Boken Kasana
District Education Officer, Gurugram
"

Dear Students, Teachers & Parents,

Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it is a journey of self-discovery, growth and empowerment. You are the future leaders, innovators and change-makers of our society. Hence, your education plays a crucial role in shaping that future.

Our district is proud of its students and I strongly believe that together we can achieve great success.

Launch of this website in PM Shri G.S.S.S Bhangrola will help parents and students effectively. If you have questions, concerns or ideas to share, don't hesitate to reach out through your teachers and school heads.

Wishing you all the best in your academic pursuits. Let's make this academic year a memorable and successful one!

Best regards,
