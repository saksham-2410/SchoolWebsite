About the School

A Legacy of
Excellence in Education

PM SHRI Govt. Sr. Sec. School, Bhangrola, established under the prestigious PM SHRI (Prime Minister Schools for Rising India) scheme, stands as a beacon of quality education in the heart of Gurugram district. Situated in the serene environs of Bhangrola village, Block Farrukhnagar, the school proudly serves over 1,117 students from the surrounding communities.

Affiliated with the Haryana Board of School Education, the institution follows a holistic educational framework that nurtures academic excellence alongside personal growth. Our campus spans an impressive 32,554 square metres, housing 20 well-ventilated classrooms, 4 modern laboratories including a computer lab, and a richly stocked library — all designed to create an enriching learning environment.

Our vision is to empower every learner with knowledge, values, and skills that enable them to contribute meaningfully to society. We believe in developing not just academically sound individuals but compassionate, responsible citizens equipped for the challenges of the 21st century.

The school takes pride in its team of 33 dedicated educators whose passion for teaching has driven students to achieve 100% scores in board examinations. With 20+ national and state recognitions, PM SHRI Govt. Sr. Sec. School, Bhangrola continues to grow in its mission to provide transformative education to every student who walks through its gates.

School Leaving Certificate (SLC) Tracking

The School Leaving Certificate is issued to students who are leaving the school. Parents/Guardians can track the status of their SLC application through the school office. Below is the process for applying for SLC:

  • Collect the SLC Application Form from the school office or download from the Downloads section.
  • Submit the duly filled form along with fee receipt and any pending dues clearance certificate.
  • SLC is typically issued within 7 working days of application submission.
  • For tracking status, contact the school office at gsssbhangrola2013@gmail.com.
  • Provisional SLC can be issued on request for admission purposes.

Note: All library books and school property must be returned before SLC is issued. Clearance from all departments is mandatory.
