Curriculum

Subject-wise syllabus follows Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE) curriculum for all classes.

Vocational

Vocational Subjects

Our Curriculum Framework

The school will implement the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 aligned curriculum under the PM SHRI scheme, emphasizing experiential learning, critical thinking, and skill development alongside traditional academics. Classes VI–X will follow a common core curriculum, while Classes XI–XII will allow specialization in Science, Arts, or Commerce streams as per student aptitude and career goals.
